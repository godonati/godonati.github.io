I'm a former designer passionate about project automation and data analysis. In my current role, I use my expertise to streamline processes and uncover valuable insights, driving growth and efficiency. Previously, as CEO, I led a team of 30 people in creating impactful content in the marketing segment. Now, I'm looking to transition my Product Manager expertise into a role focusing on Blockchain, and Web3. I foster collaboration and have a goofy sense of humor. Let's connect!

As a Product Manager, my role is to oversee the development and success of a product from concept to launch. I work closely with different teams—like engineering, design, and marketing—to ensure the product meets user needs and business goals. My responsibilities include defining the product vision, prioritizing features, managing the roadmap, and making strategic decisions based on user feedback and market trends. Essentially, I act as a bridge between various teams to deliver a product that solves real problems and drives business value.

  • -What inspired you to make a career change into this field?
    I was inspired to move into Product Management because I wanted to have a greater impact on shaping the direction and success of products, rather than just contributing to one part of the process. My background in video production showed me the value of aligning creativity with business goals, and I wanted to leverage that experience to manage and build products from a strategic perspective.
  • What did you enjoy most about creating the “Via Production” product?
    What I enjoyed most about creating the "Via Production" product was the opportunity to streamline the video production process, enabling teams to produce high-quality content more efficiently. It was fulfilling to see how empowering editors with creative freedom led to innovative videos that truly resonated with the marketing goals of the company.
  • What did you enjoy about creating your portfolio project?
    I enjoyed creating my portfolio project because it allowed me to showcase my skills and experiences in a structured way, highlighting the impact of my work on product development. It was rewarding to synthesize my achievements into a cohesive narrative that illustrates my journey and growth as a Product Manager.
  • With regards to your career and skills, what are your hopes for the future?
    I hope to continue expanding my expertise in Product Management by taking on more complex projects that challenge me and allow for innovation. Ultimately, I aspire to lead cross-functional teams in developing products that make a meaningful difference in users' lives while driving business success.
